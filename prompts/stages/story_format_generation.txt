You are working in the story format generation stage.
This stage does not create the final story.
It creates a reusable story format blueprint that explains how story elements should be combined.
A format is a structural pattern, not a finished headline.

A strong format usually includes:
- who the storyteller is
- where the story happened
- what the initial situation was
- what made it emotionally charged
- what made it recognizable
- what absurd or surprising escalation happened
- what twist, reveal, or payoff exists
- what kind of audience feeling it should trigger

The format should create enough structure for later headline generation, while still leaving room for variation.
