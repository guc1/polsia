You are working in the hook generation stage.
This stage creates the opening hook for a story.
The hook should make people want to continue, but should not destroy the later payoff by over-explaining the whole plot.
The hook has two parts:
1. the initial hook line, which is the most important first sentence
2. the rest of the opening hook, which deepens curiosity and prepares the story

This stage is still story-preparation, not final short-form script writing.
